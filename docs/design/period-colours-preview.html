The Four Periods

Period I
Youth & Formation
1828 – 1851
Childhood at Yasnaya Polyana, orphaned young, university dropout, restless moral self-examination.
Period II
The Soldier & Sinner
1851 – 1862
Caucasus campaign, Sevastopol, Petersburg literary fame, gambling, dissipation, travel in Europe.
Period III
The Great Works
1862 – 1880
Marriage, Yasnaya Polyana school, War and Peace, Anna Karenina — world fame at its height.
Period IV
The Prophet
1880 – 1910
Conversion, renunciation, Christian anarchism, excommunication, the Tolstoyan movement, death at Astapovo.
